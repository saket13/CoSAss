Corona Self Assesment

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many hospitals and test centres have exceeded their treatment capacities and suffer from a lack of resources. Lots of time and majority of resources are being spent on testing for the virus alone. CoSAss helps you to find out probability of someone having infection on the basis of symptoms. This will help test centres to prioritize the tests under limited testing capacity. CoSAss works not only to help "flatten the curve" by helping prioritize the tests, but also to "raise the line" that symbolizes healthcare system capacity. Click below to take the test.

How to remain safe during COVID 19 !!!

Clean Your Hands

Wash your hands often with soap and water before eating, food handling, after leaving the laavatory, after contact with bodily secretions, after coughing or sneezing, and after touching the used tissue paper.

Cover Your Mouth While Sneezing

Cover your mouth and nose with tissue paper or wear a mask if possible if you are sneezing or in public place.

Say No To Handshakes

Try to avoid handshakes as much as possible and do Namaste by hands or say Hello if necessary.

Maintain Social Distancing

If you are having symptoms stay home; do not take children with symptoms to child care
